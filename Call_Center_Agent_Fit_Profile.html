Call Center Agent Ideal Profile

This survey collects your professional insights to help us define what a truly successful Call Center Agent looks like in the South African context. Your input will be combined with psychometric data analysis to build an Ideal Profile. An Ideal Profile is a benchmark that describes the cognitive abilities and behavioural traits most predictive of high performance in the role, enabling more precise and evidence-based candidate matching.
